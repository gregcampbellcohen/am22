About Clean and Healthy New York

Clean and Healthy New York is an environmental health advocacy organization committed to building a just and healthy society where toxic chemicals are unthinkable. We are proud to provide leadership to two major statewide disease-prevention coalitions: the JustGreen Partnership, a team of 50 organizations representing over a million New Yorkers, and Lead Free Kids New York, a coalition of individuals and organizations working to end childhood lead exposure in New York. Key priorities include:

  • Safe Personal Care and Cosmetics Act
    (S.3331 Rivera, et al./A.143 Gottfried, et al.)
  • Lead Disclosure at Point of Sale
    (S.2142A Kavanagh, et al./A.6608A Rivera, et al.)
  • PFAS in Common Apparel
    (S.6291 Hoylman/A.7063 Fahy, et al.)
  • Clearing the Toxic Air
    (S.4371B Biaggi, et al./A.6150 Septimo, et al.)
